<p><p>The submarine communication cables market comprises two primary types: unarmoured and armoured cables. Unarmoured submarine communication cables are lightweight and flexible, typically used in shallower waters or less hazardous environments where physical protection from external threats is not critical. In contrast, armoured submarine communication cables feature added protective layers, making them robust and suitable for deployment in deeper waters or areas prone to fishing activities, underwater hazards, or harsh environmental conditions. Both types are essential for global data transmission.</p></p>
