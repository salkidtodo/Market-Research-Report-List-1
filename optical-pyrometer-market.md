<p><p>An optical pyrometer is a non-contact temperature measurement device that determines temperature by measuring the intensity of thermal radiation emitted by an object. These instruments are widely used in industries such as metal processing, glass manufacturing, and ceramics, where high-temperature measurements are essential. The optical pyrometer operates based on the principle that the emitted radiation intensity is proportional to the temperature of the object, enabling accurate measurements even in extreme conditions.</p><p>The Optical Pyrometer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for precise temperature measurement in various industrial applications.
