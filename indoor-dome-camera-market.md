<p><p>The indoor dome camera market features three primary types: Fixed Lens, Varifocal Lens, and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom). Fixed lens cameras offer a consistent field of view, making them ideal for specific monitoring tasks. Varifocal lens cameras allow users to adjust the focal length and field of view, providing flexibility for varied surveillance needs. PTZ cameras can pan, tilt, and zoom remotely, enabling comprehensive area coverage and detail retrieval, making them suitable for dynamic environments requiring active monitoring.</p></p>
