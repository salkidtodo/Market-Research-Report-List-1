<p><p>The UV disinfection equipment market includes several types, each utilizing specific technologies. Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection uses low-pressure mercury lamps emitting UV light to kill microbes in water or air. High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection leverages powerful UV lamps for rapid and effective pathogen inactivation, suitable for large-scale applications. Ozone UV Disinfection combines UV light and ozone generation to enhance disinfection efficacy, particularly in water treatment, by breaking down contaminants and ensuring higher sterilization levels.</p></p>
