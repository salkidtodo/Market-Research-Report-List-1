<p><ul><li>Cantilever Piezoelectric Ceramic Sound</li><li>Screen Exciter Sound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen sound technology encompasses various types, including cantilever piezoelectric ceramic sound and screen exciter sound. Cantilever piezoelectric ceramics utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, producing sound directly from the screen surface. Screen exciters, on the other hand, are external devices that attach to the screen to create vibrations, enhancing sound quality while offering a compact design. Both technologies prioritize immersive audio experiences, making them popular in applications like home theater systems and commercial displays.</p></p>
