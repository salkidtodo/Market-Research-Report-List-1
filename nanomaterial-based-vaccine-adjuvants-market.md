<p><p>The nanomaterial-based vaccine adjuvants market is divided into two main types: organic nano adjuvants and inorganic nano adjuvants. Organic nano adjuvants, derived from natural compounds such as lipids and polysaccharides, enhance immune responses while being biocompatible and less toxic. In contrast, inorganic nano adjuvants, typically composed of metal oxides or silica, offer stability and controlled release properties, improving vaccine efficacy. Both types play a crucial role in developing safer and more effective vaccines by enhancing immune stimulation.</p></p>
