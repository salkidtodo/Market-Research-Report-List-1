<p><p>A freeze dryer, or lyophilizer, is a specialized equipment used to remove moisture from products, effectively preserving them while maintaining their structure, taste, and nutritional value. As consumers seek convenient and long-lasting food options, the market for freeze-dried foods has expanded, with applications in outdoor and emergency preparedness sectors.
