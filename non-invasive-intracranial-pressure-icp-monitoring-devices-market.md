<p><strong>The Non invasive Intracranial Pressure ICP Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Signal Detection</li><li>Blood Flow Signal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-invasive intracranial pressure (ICP) monitoring devices employ different technologies to assess cranial pressure without surgical intervention. The sound signal detection method utilizes acoustic waves to measure changes in pressure by analyzing transmitted sound waves through cranial tissues. Blood flow signal detection, on the other hand, gauges ICP by monitoring cerebral blood flow patterns, utilizing techniques like near-infrared spectroscopy. Both approaches aim to provide real-time, accurate assessments, enhancing patient safety and comfort while facilitating timely medical interventions.</p></p>
