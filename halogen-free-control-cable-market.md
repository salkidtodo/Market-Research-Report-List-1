<p><ul><li>LSZH</li><li>FRNC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The halogen-free control cable market is categorized primarily into Low Smoke Zero Halogen (LSZH) and Flame Retardant Non-Corroding (FRNC) types. LSZH cables minimize toxic gas emissions and smoke during combustion, making them ideal for enclosed spaces like tunnels and buildings. FRNC cables also reduce harmful emissions but focus on flame retardancy without corrosive effects, ensuring safety in electrical installations. Both types cater to industries demanding high safety standards, especially in urban environments and sensitive applications.</p></p>
