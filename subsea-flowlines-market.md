<p><p>The subsea flowlines market is categorized into low, medium, and high-pressure segments based on the operational pressures they can handle. Low-pressure flowlines are designed for applications with pressures typically below 5,000 psi, suitable for shallow waters. Medium-pressure flowlines range from 5,000 to 10,000 psi, often used in deeper offshore contexts. High-pressure flowlines, exceeding 10,000 psi, are utilized in deepwater and ultra-deepwater markets, accommodating the harsher conditions and ensuring efficient transport of hydrocarbons from subsea production units to surface facilities.</p></p>
