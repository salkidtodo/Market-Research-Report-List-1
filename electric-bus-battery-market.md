<p><p>The electric bus battery market features various types of batteries based on their chemical composition. NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) batteries offer high energy density and efficiency, making them suitable for long-range buses. LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries prioritize safety and longevity, ideal for urban transit. LCO (Lithium Cobalt Oxide) batteries provide high energy output but have a shorter lifespan, while LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) batteries balance safety and performance, catering to diverse operational needs in electric bus fleets.</p></p>
