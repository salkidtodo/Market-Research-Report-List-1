<p><p>The construction equipment tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires, filled with air, offer better shock absorption and enhanced traction on uneven surfaces. Solid tires, made from rubber, provide durability and resistance to punctures, making them ideal for rough environments. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering flexibility and longer lifespan while minimizing risk of damage. Each type serves specific needs, ensuring optimal performance for various construction equipment applications.</p></p>
