<p><p>The yeast cell wall extract market is primarily categorized into two types: yeast extract and autolyzed yeast. Yeast extract is derived from yeast cells through enzymatic hydrolysis, providing essential nutrients, amino acids, and vitamins, often used in food and nutritional products. Both types are valued for their nutritional and functional properties in various applications.</p></p>
