<p><strong>The Tricone Drill Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCI Drill Bits</li><li>Milled Tooth Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tricone drill bits market primarily features two types: TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and Milled Tooth drill bits. TCI drill bits utilize tough tungsten carbide inserts for enhanced durability and performance in hard rock formations, making them ideal for drilling in challenging environments. Milled Tooth drill bits, with teeth made from steel that are milled into the bit body, are better suited for softer formations, offering cost-effectiveness and efficient drilling in less abrasive conditions. Both types cater to diverse drilling needs.</p></p>
