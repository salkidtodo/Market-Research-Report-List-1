<p><ul><li>Flyback</li><li>Forward</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The isolated DC-DC converter market primarily includes two types: Flyback and Forward converters. Flyback converters use a transformer for isolation and energy storage, allowing for higher voltage outputs with simpler designs, making them ideal for low to medium power applications. In contrast, Forward converters also utilize a transformer but offer improved efficiency and performance for higher power applications, providing better regulation and lower electromagnetic interference. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
