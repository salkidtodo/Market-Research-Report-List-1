<p><p>The soy-based sauce market is primarily divided into two types: brewed soy-based sauce and blended soy-based sauce. Brewed soy-based sauce is traditionally made through fermentation, imparting a rich, complex flavor profile that enhances dishes. In contrast, blended soy-based sauce combines brewed soy sauce with other ingredients, such as additives and flavor enhancers, resulting in a more consistent taste and lower production costs. Both types cater to diverse culinary applications, appealing to a wide range of consumers and cuisines.</p></p>
