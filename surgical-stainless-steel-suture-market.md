<p><ul><li>Monofilament Steel Suture</li><li>Multifilament Steel Suture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Stainless Steel Suture market comprises two main types: monofilament and multifilament steel sutures. Monofilament steel sutures consist of a single strand, providing minimal tissue trauma and a smooth passage through tissues, making them ideal for delicate procedures. Conversely, multifilament steel sutures consist of multiple strands twisted or braided together, offering enhanced flexibility and knot security, which is beneficial for high-tension areas. Both types are valued for their strength, biocompatibility, and resistance to infection in surgical applications.</p></p>
