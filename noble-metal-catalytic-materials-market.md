<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Catalysis</li><li>Heterogeneous Catalysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Noble metal catalytic materials market encompasses two main types: homogeneous and heterogeneous catalysis. Homogeneous catalysis involves catalysts dissolved in the same phase as the reactants, allowing for uniform mixing and efficiency, commonly used in organic reactions. In contrast, heterogeneous catalysis features catalysts that exist in a different phase, often solid, while the reactants are in gas or liquid form. This type allows for easier separation of products and catalysts, making it ideal for industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing reaction rates and efficiency.</p></p>
