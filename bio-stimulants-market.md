<p><p>The bio stimulants market is categorized into three main types: humic-based, amino-based, and fulvic-based products. Humic-based bio stimulants enhance soil health and nutrient uptake by improving microbial activity and soil structure. Amino-based bio stimulants provide essential amino acids that promote plant growth and stress tolerance. Fulvic-based bio stimulants facilitate nutrient transport and enhance soil's nutrient-holding capacity, supporting healthier plant development. Each type plays a critical role in promoting sustainable agriculture by improving plant resilience and productivity.</p></p>
