<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Physical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive sway bars market can be categorized into active and physical types. Active sway bars utilize advanced technologies to dynamically adjust stiffness and improve vehicle stability during cornering, enhancing overall handling and ride comfort. In contrast, physical sway bars are traditional, static components that connect the vehicle's suspension and reduce body roll during turns, providing a more straightforward and cost-effective solution. Both types serve to enhance vehicle performance, but they cater to different needs and technological advancements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
