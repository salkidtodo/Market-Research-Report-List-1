<p><strong>The Railway Wheel-Sets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboard Bearings Arrangement</li><li>Inboard Bearing Arrangement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway wheel-sets market is categorized into two main types based on bearing arrangements: outboard bearings and inboard bearings. Outboard bearing arrangements position the bearings outside the wheel and axle assembly, providing easier maintenance and reduced friction. In contrast, inboard bearing arrangements place the bearings inside the wheel-sets, allowing for a more compact design, enhancing overall structural stability. Both arrangements impact performance, maintenance, and operational efficiency, influencing the choice depending on specific railway system requirements.</p></p>
