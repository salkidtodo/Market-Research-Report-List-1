<p><p>The Neuromodulation and Neurostimulation Pain Management Devices market includes technologies designed to alter nerve activity and alleviate pain. Spinal Cord Stimulators (SCS) are implanted devices that send electrical impulses to the spinal cord, disrupting pain signals before they reach the brain. On the other hand, Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) Devices are non-invasive units that apply low-voltage electrical currents through the skin to stimulate nerves and reduce pain perception. Both approaches provide alternative solutions for chronic pain management.</p></p>
