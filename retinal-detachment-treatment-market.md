<p><strong>The Retinal Detachment Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Retinopexy</li><li>Scleral Buckle Surgery</li><li>Vitrectomy</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retinal detachment treatment market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic Retinopexy** involves injecting a gas bubble into the eye to push the retina back into place. </p><p>2. **Scleral Buckle Surgery** utilizes a flexible silicone band to indent the eye wall, securing the retina. </p><p>3. **Vitrectomy** entails surgically removing the vitreous gel to alleviate traction on the retina and facilitate reattachment. </p><p>4. **Others** may include laser therapy and other less common techniques. Each method has distinct applications based on the detachment's severity and type.</p></p>
