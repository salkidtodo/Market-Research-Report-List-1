<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) equipment encompasses various techniques for evaluating materials and structures without causing damage. X-Ray testing provides internal images to identify hidden flaws. Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect imperfections within a material. Magnetic particle testing reveals surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials through magnetic fields. Penetrant flaw detection identifies surface-breaking defects using liquid penetrants. Eddy current testing detects conductivity and thickness variations in conductive materials. Other methods may include acoustic emission and thermal imaging techniques.</p></p>
