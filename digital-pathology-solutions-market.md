<p><p>The digital pathology solutions market encompasses two primary types: cytopathology and histopathology. Cytopathology focuses on the examination of individual cells or small clusters of cells, often used for early cancer detection and diagnosing infections. In contrast, histopathology involves the study of tissues and organ sections to diagnose diseases, especially cancer, by examining cellular structures. Both types leverage digital imaging and analysis technologies to enhance accuracy, streamline workflows, and improve diagnostic efficiency in pathology practices.</p></p>
