<p><ul><li>Vertical Pipeline Pump</li><li>Horizontal Pipeline Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipeline pump market is primarily categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal pipeline pumps. Vertical pipeline pumps are designed to operate in confined spaces, featuring a compact design that utilizes a vertical orientation to minimize footprint. They are commonly used in applications with limited floor space or when suction lift conditions are required. In contrast, horizontal pipeline pumps are more widely used for their straightforward design and simpler maintenance, making them suitable for a variety of industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
