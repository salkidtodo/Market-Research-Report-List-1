<p><ul><li>SPST</li><li>SPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS analog switch market includes various types, primarily Single Pole Single Throw (SPST) and Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switches. SPST switches control a single signal path, allowing or interrupting the flow of analog signals, making them ideal for simple on-off applications. SPDT switches, on the other hand, manage two signal paths, enabling the selection between two different sources or destinations. These switches are widely used in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and industrial applications, contributing to efficient signal routing and enhanced performance.</p></p>
