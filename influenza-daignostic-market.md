<p><ul><li>Rapid Flu Detection Test</li><li>Virus Detection</li><li>Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) Test</li><li>Serological Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The influenza diagnostic market encompasses various testing types, each serving different purposes. Rapid flu detection tests quickly identify influenza viruses using nasal or throat swabs, providing results within hours. Virus detection involves culturing the virus from patient samples, offering a more definitive diagnosis. Direct fluorescent antibody (DFA) tests detect viral antigens in respiratory specimens through fluorescent staining. Serological testing measures antibodies in the blood, indicating past infection or vaccination, and helps in epidemiological studies and assessing immunity levels.</p></p>
